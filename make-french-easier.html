Make French Easier

French is a beautiful language. Many people, including native French speakers,
find the French language to be unnecessarily complicated. I want to make French easier. For everyone.

How will you make French easier?

This isn't an easy thing to do.
First things first, not everything in this language will be changed. Some things
will definitely remain the same, such as «avril» or pronouns.
Unnecessary letters (ex. le gâteau), maybe sentence structures will be changed and grammar rules removed.
